About The Event

A cruise on the sailing yacht with the Croatia Yacht Club means that, together with a group of between six and ten people, you will be able to explore Croatia's amazing archipelago on a 35 to 53-meter sailboat.

The experience of sailing does not matter when you have a shipowner who takes responsibility for you as a crew as well as the boat while the hostess arranges food and ensures that your well-being is at the top. Onboard you choose which commitment you want in the sailing and you can simply choose whether you want to just sunbathe or learn more about sailing and navigation.

All nights are spent with access to the restaurant and you will also visit a few villages in the archipelago during the week. The route for the week is planned in advance but of course there is the possibility of changes depending on the wishes but also the weather conditions. Port of departure varies depending on where the boat's home port is located.

In the tabmenu you can choose which package you are interested in!

What's included in the trip?

The price of the trip includes first and foremost part of the cabin on the sailboat, as well as flights from Stockholm or Gothenburg and also the transfer from the airport to the boat. In addition to this, breakfast and lunch are also prepared by your hostess and boarded. Also included are the port, diesel and boat packages included..
