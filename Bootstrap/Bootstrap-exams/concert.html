About the Event

No less than Iron Maiden will come to Sweden next summer with his Legacy Of The Beast World Tour for a concert at the Tele2 Arena in Stockholm on Friday, June 1, after which to enter the Sweden Rock Festival - "the biggest booking in the history of the festival".

Iron Maiden, one of the world's absolute biggest hardcore bands and one of its best markets in Sweden, is launching this summer on a new world tour called Legacy Of The Beast. The European part of the tour will start in late May, reaching Sweden at the beginning of June.
