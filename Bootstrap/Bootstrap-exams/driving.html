About the Event

Your experience begins by arriving at the place of your driving in the morning and welcomed by the instructor. Together you go through the car and the instructor shows the driving technique and where all the controls are in place. When you feel comfortable with the car, it's time for you to slide into the driver's seat and change position as a sports car driver.

You notice at once that you are sitting behind the wheel of a top-class supercar. A light pressure on the accelerator gives an immediate response from the very powerful engine. You then run a suitable loop along with the instructor. If you did not like sports cars before you will get it after this!
