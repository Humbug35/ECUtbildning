About Parachute

For parachute jumping, you have to see the ground from the plane, therefore, jumping is set for full clouds, rain or winds over 10m / s. It is therefore good if you call and check the weather any / a few hours before. In bad weather, rebooking will take place until another date.

Preparation

Minimum length 140 cm and max weight 100 kg. In addition to the restrictions you should be healthy and you are under 18 years of age, the goalkeeper's signature is required and you are at least 14 years old. Sobriety is of course a requirement!

Parachute

You will be received by a tandem pilot or jump leader who is responsible for jumping during the day. Then a review follows, where the instructor goes through all the necessary equipment, outfit from the airplane, the free fall, the screen and landing. You then take space in the plane flying up to about 4000 meters within about 25 minutes as you can enjoy the view and walk through the jump with your tandem pilot once more.
